Thanks for spending sometime to know me. Although I have given a small introduction about myself on front page, I will again give a small background about myself here. I am a Software Engineer by profession, which brings food to my table and ideas in my head. My life has been a good roller coaster journey so far. I may appear very serious types at first, but once you know me, you will realize that I am 'seriously fun' to hang out with.
