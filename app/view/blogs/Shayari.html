Shayari

This is my attempt with pen, completely 'filmy' style. ;-)

Kash..

Kash unse Itni mohabbat na hoti, na Milne ka intezaar na Yeh judai hoti..
Kar dete unse Hal e dil jo bayan, khuda bhi khush hota aur woh meri bahon main hoti..
Kash unse Itni mohabbat na hoti, na tadapta dil na yeh bechaini hoti..
Rakhte har waqt unhe palko par betha kar, dil hota unka magar usme dhadkane humari hoti..
Kash unse Itni mohabbat na hoti, na dooriyo ka gum na ankhen Yu nam hoti..
woh ho na sakenge humare Yeh malum jo hota, na karta unse utna pyar na aisi Yeh meri halat hoti..
Kash unse Itni mohabbat na hoti.. Kash unse Itni mohabbat na hoti..

Abhi Baki Hai..

Hatho main chalakta jaam abhi baki hai.. Is mohabbat ke kai imtihaan abhi baki hai..
Milte hai woh hamse chup chup kar nashe main yuhi.. Unhe galey lagane ka armaan abhi baki hai..
Pitey hai unki yaad main hum kyuki.. Madhoshi main sanam ka deedar abhi baki hai..
Log kehte hai itni na piya karo marr jaogey... Kaise chodu yeh Jaam ishq ka iqraar abhi baki hai..
Chada hai kyu kambhakt yeh ishq sharab ki tarah... Unsee yeh duri ka gum abhi baki hai...
Thodi aur pila de mujhko ko aye saqi.. Kal hosh main aakar jeene ka anjaam abhi baki hai..

Maar Gayi..

Kuch unki adaa ne luta, kuch unki inayat maar gayi,
Hum raaz-e-dil keh na paaye, chup rehne ki ye adaat maar gayi,
Tamanna to thi unko jee bhar k dekhne ki, par hume ankho ki ye sharafat maar gayi,
Dono se hi shikwa hume, ilzam lagaye kis par,
Kuch humne apne aapko barbaad kiya aur kuch hume kismat maar gayi...

Dosti..

Tham jati hai zindagi jab khayal unka aata hai,
Chehra hi nahi unke sath bitaya har lamha yaad aata hai,
Unhe to pata bhi nahi ki kitna pyar karte hai unse,
Kash yeh sach bhi ankhon main bayan ho jata,
Keh na paye unse yeh gum to hamesha rahega,
Lekin unki dosti khone ka ehsas hi sab kuch mere dil main dafn kaar jata hai..

Chhod Gaye..

Wafa ki umeed unse kyu kare jo tadapne ke liye chhod gaye,
Pass itne aye ki diwana banaya fir muh moda aur chale gaye,
Meri ankhon main ansu unke dhoke ka nahi usne bichadne ke gum ka hai,
Unhe fir bhi taras na aya hum par aur hum apni sanse unke liye chhod gaye...

Umeed..

Tujhse 1 mulakat kafi nahi dil ko samjhane ke liye,
Woh to intezar kar raha hai tujhe apna banane ke liye,
Ho na sako agar tum humarey koi gum nahi,
Par thodi si dil main jagah rakhna apne is diwane ke liye..

Yaad..

Yaad teri jab ayi to dil thama par,
Teri judai ka ehsas dila gaya gum humey,
Socha ki jam banakar pi jau tujhe,
Par nasha to pehle hi kar diya tere diwane pan ne,
Bahey fela kar khada hoon tujhe galey lagane ko,
Par duri ne mita diya isey bhi mere mann main,
Chaha ki apni hasti hi milta du lekin,
Jaan to pehle hi le li thi is akele pan ne..

Rishtey..

Dard hota hai dil main jab zakhmo ko chupate hai,
Panah mangte hai unse jo us dard ko aur badhate hai,
Yeh saja mili hai humey dil lagane ki,
Ke dil main rehne wale hi humare dil ko dukhate hai ...

Judai..

Aaj fir ankho main ansu hai ke baat adhuri reh gayi..
Jindagi pass humare aakar fir humse bhichad gayi..
Tujhse shikwa kare aye sanam ya us upar wale se..
Baho main aakar meri fir humey woh akela chodd gayi...
Main to sochta hi reh Gaya ki unhe apna banau...
Woh to mere dil main jagah bana kar kisi aur ki ho gayi...

Hal..

Jo haal kiya zindagi ne mera..
woh kisi insaan ka na kare..
Apna banakar koi apne sey juda na kare..
Dua karte hai khuda sey tumharey liye aye dost..
Ho tumhe pyar jis say woh kabhi kisi aur sey pyar na kare..

Khamoshi..

Katl kar gayi humara khamoshi ye teri..
Tu to chala gaya reh gayi dil main arzoo meri..
Shabdo sey kya bayan karey tumhe in zajbaton ko..
Ishq main tuhmare maar gaye hote to yakeen aata..

Muskurahat..

Jindagi main jo na chaho wahi har bar milta hai..
Ishq main kaha har waqt ikhtiyaar milta hai..
Woh kehte hum aajkal muskuratey nahi..
Unhe kya samjhaye Yeh judai ka gum jo bar bar milta hai..

Intezaar..

Woh is baat se khush hai ki thoda waqt sath bitayenge..
Hum is baat se pareshan ki yeh intezar kab khatam hoga..
